Top 10 Popular Dog Breeds In India

There is nothing quite like having a dog in the house. Dogs shower you with unconditional love and affection. What's more, they even bring your stress levels down—something so hard to find in the human world! If you are toying with the idea of getting a dog, you will want to know which are the most popular dog breeds in India, and what their personalities are like. Let’s have a look at all of the characteristics that can help you make an informed decision before bringing a furry one home!

Labrador   Golden-Retriever   German-shepherd   Beagle   Rottweilers   Shih-Tzus   Pomeranian   Cocker-spaniel   Boxer   Poodle

1. Labrador

Labrador Retrievers (commonly known as Labradors) are sweet-faced, loveable and adorable! One of the most popular dog breeds in India, Labradors are outgoing, friendly, and have enough love in them for the whole family! The Labrador, a great socialiser, rarely has a bone to pick with neighbouring dogs.

Life expectancy 10-14 years

Suitable for Indian climate

Height- Male 62cm/Female 60 cm

2. Golden Retriever

Originally from Scotland, Golden Retrievers are in demand as they are caring, intelligent and very obedient. They make good watchdogs, are an active dog breed in India, and can be trained for dog shows and competitions. Golden Retrievers puppies are sure to make your heart melt! Keep them active and entertained with these dog toys.

Life expectancy-10-12 years

Suitable for Indian climate

Height: Male/Female up to 61/56 cm

3. German Shepherd

German Shepherd dogs have wolf-like looks, but they are incredibly loving, gentle and instinctively protective. One of the more active dog breeds in India, they make perfect watch dogs. German Shepherd puppies are also trained as search and rescue dogs for their keen senses, intelligence and loyalty.

Life span-around 11 years

Suitable for Indian climate

Height – Male/Female up to 65/60 cm

4. The Beagle

Beagles are small-medium in size, but make up for it with their big hearts and personalities! Merry and super fun-loving, Beagles are gaining popularity because of their sheer charm. They are super curious explorers and are one of the more active dog breeds in India

Life expectancy: 12-15 years

Height: Male/Female 36/33cm

Suitable for Indian climate

5. Rottweilers

Rottweilers are powerful, dominant and yet very loyal and friendly dogs. They have a short coat and tend to shed fur frequently. Lovely as family pets, and are super intelligent during training sessions.

Life expectancy-8-10 years

Suitable for Indian Climate

Height: Male/Female up to 69/63 cm

6. The Shih Tzus

One of the small dog breeds in India, Shih Tzus have been companion dogs to even Chinese royalty! Their name means ‘little lion’ in Mandarin, and have always been considered warm, gentle yet agile and playful furry ones who love to cuddle! Explore dog food best suited for small breeds.

Lifespan-10-16years

Height: 23sms-27cms

Not suitable for Indian climate

7. The Pomeranian

Pomeranians are one the small dog breeds in India. They are affectionate and friendly, and it is easy to teach them tricks. A Pomeranian can be your best friend if you can keep up with their energetic, curious selves!

Lifespan 12-16 years

Suitable for Indian climate

8. The cocker spaniel

Cocker Spaniels were initially bred as hunting dogs, and are a crossbreed of the American Cocker Spaniel and the English Cocker Spaniel. They have a silky coat and floppy ears. Their medium-size, adorable looks and smart minds make them easy to train, great playmates and the best dog for home in India! Check out these dog treats, perfect for training.

Life span-12 years

Height: 38 cm

Suitable for Indian climate

9. Boxer

Boxer dogs make lovely pets, one of the best dogs for home in India as they are effortless to train and maintain. Their square and squat muzzle make the boxer dog breed very adorable. Boxer breeds have loving eyes, a wrinkled forehead and a curious expression.

Life expectancy-10-12 years

Height: male/female 63 cm- 60cm

10. Poodle

Poodles have an aristocratic air about them, but they are anything but aloof. This affectionate, playful and fun-loving breed is ideal if you are looking for a cuddly companion, especially for families.

Lifespan-12-15years

Height: 45cms-62cms

Suitable for Indian climate
